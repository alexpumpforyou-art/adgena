# AdGena — Промпты для генерации примеров (thumbnails)

> Используй эти промпты в стороннем сервисе (Midjourney, DALL-E, Flux и т.д.)
> Для каждого концепта нужна одна картинка-пример с любым товаром
> Формат: квадрат (1:1), размер ~512x512 или больше

---

## 📸 ФОТО-КОНЦЕПЦИИ

Эти концепции появляются как кнопки на вкладке "Фото" в дашборде.
Каждая концепция используется для разных категорий товаров.

---

### 1. `on-model` — На модели
**Категории:** Одежда, Аксессуары
**Суть:** Модель носит/демонстрирует товар. Fashion-фотография.

**Промпт (EN — используй его):**
```
Professional fashion photography. Show a model wearing a stylish jacket. Model standing in a fashion studio. Focus on fit and fabric texture. Natural, not overly posed. Lighting: soft studio light. Shallow depth of field. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 2. `in-store` — Как в магазине
**Категории:** Одежда
**Суть:** Товар на вешалке/манекене в бутике.

**Промпт:**
```
Professional in-store product photography. A premium hoodie neatly displayed on a hanger in a stylish boutique setting. Blurred store interior background. Neat presentation. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 3. `flat-lay` — Раскладка сверху
**Категории:** Одежда, Аксессуары, Еда, Детские, Прочее
**Суть:** Вид строго сверху (90°), аккуратная раскладка с аксессуарами.

**Промпт:**
```
Professional flat lay photography, strictly top-down view. A pair of sneakers laid out on a clean marble surface, shot from directly above (90° overhead). Surrounded by 2-3 complementary props (sunglasses, watch, phone). Neat, symmetrical composition. Even, soft lighting. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 4. `studio` — Каталог (студийно)
**Категории:** ВСЕ (универсальный)
**Суть:** Товар на белом фоне, классическая каталожная съёмка.

**Промпт:**
```
Professional studio catalog photography. A wireless headphone on pure white seamless background. Three-point studio lighting. Contact shadow. Centered, filling 80-85% of frame. Tack-sharp focus. No text, no props. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 5. `in-use` — В использовании
**Категории:** Косметика, Гаджеты, Дом, Детские, Прочее
**Суть:** Человек естественно использует товар. Lifestyle.

**Промпт:**
```
Professional lifestyle photography. Person holding a sleek smartphone in a modern workspace. Person looks natural, not overly posed. Product is the clear focal point. Warm natural light. Shallow depth of field (f/2.8). Medium shot. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 6. `in-context` — В окружении
**Категории:** Аксессуары, Еда, Косметика, Гаджеты, Прочее
**Суть:** Товар в стилизованной обстановке, с 2-3 предметами рядом.

**Промпт:**
```
Professional styled product photography. A luxury watch placed on a polished wooden surface in an elegant vanity setting. 2-3 complementary props nearby (leather notebook, pen). Soft natural light. Warm, magazine-worthy atmosphere. Product is the focal point. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 7. `close-up` — Крупный план
**Категории:** Гаджеты, Дом
**Суть:** Макро-детали: кнопки, текстура, швы, экраны.

**Промпт:**
```
Macro product photography, close-up. Capture one key detail of a mechanical keyboard: switches, keycaps, LED backlighting. Shallow DOF, blurred background. Dramatic side lighting to highlight texture. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 100mm macro lens.
```

---

### 8. `in-interior` — В интерьере
**Категории:** Дом
**Суть:** Товар в реальном интерьере (комната, кухня, сад).

**Промпт:**
```
Interior product photography. A stylish floor lamp naturally placed in a modern living room — in its proper place, like a real interior. Soft window light. Modern stylish Scandinavian interior. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, professional color grading, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 9. `plated` — Сервировка
**Категории:** Еда
**Суть:** Еда красиво подана — на тарелке, в бокале.

**Промпт:**
```
Professional food photography. A gourmet pasta dish beautifully plated on a ceramic plate on a wooden table. Fresh ingredients nearby (basil, tomatoes, olive oil). Warm appetizing lighting. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, food photography style, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm lens.
```

---

### 10. `texture` — Текстура крупно
**Категории:** Косметика
**Суть:** Макро текстуры крема, масла, сыворотки.

**Промпт:**
```
Macro texture photography of beauty product. Close-up of a rich moisturizer cream — swatch on clean white surface. Showing creamy consistency and pearl-like color. Soft diffused light. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution, high-end commercial photography, macro lens.
```

## 🎨 РЕКЛАМНЫЕ КОНЦЕПЦИИ (вкладка "Реклама")

---

### 11. `ad-sale` — Акция / Sale
**Суть:** Яркий продающий баннер с ценой, CTA, декором.

**Промпт:**
```
Create a premium, visually rich high-conversion SALE advertising banner. Product: stylish running sneakers. Hero product on the left ~45% of frame, slightly tilted for energy, floating above a soft shadow with a glowing halo behind it. Right half: headline "SPECIAL OFFER" in bold condensed white sans-serif. Background: energetic gradient (coral to deep red), diagonal light rays, bokeh circles. Price badge: bright yellow circle with "2990₽". CTA button "Buy Now" in yellow pill shape. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution.
```

---

### 12. `ad-premium` — Премиум
**Суть:** Люксовый баннер, тёмные тона, минимум декора, элегантность.

**Промпт:**
```
Create a premium luxury advertising banner. Product: elegant leather handbag. Product slightly off-center, large and elegant with generous negative space. Subtle contact shadow, soft reflection on glossy surface. Headline "PREMIUM CHOICE" in elegant high-contrast sans-serif. Background: smooth dark gradient (graphite to black), subtle vignette, brushed metal texture. Soft cinematic lighting, elegant rim light. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution.
```

---

### 13. `ad-fresh` — Свежий / Lifestyle
**Суть:** Светлый, воздушный, дружелюбный — для everyday товаров.

**Промпт:**
```
Create a fresh, airy lifestyle advertising banner. Product: natural skincare bottle. Product centered, clean and approachable, surrounded by soft organic leaf shapes and airy negative space. Headline "FRESH PICK" in friendly rounded sans-serif. Background: soft pastel gradient (mint to cream), clean daylight, gentle shadows. Soft natural daylight lighting. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution.
```

---

### 14. `ad-minimal` — Минимализм (Apple-style)
**Суть:** Чисто белый фон, товар по центру, тонкий шрифт. Ничего лишнего.

**Промпт:**
```
Create a minimalist, premium advertising creative in Apple/MUJI style. Product: wireless earbuds case. Product perfectly centered, occupying ~60% of the frame height. Floating on pure white (#FFFFFF) background with a very subtle contact shadow. Product name "AirPods Pro" in thin elegant sans-serif, dark gray, centered above. Background: pure white, no gradients, absolute minimalism. Even diffused studio lighting. Ultra-clean, gallery-quality. Photorealistic, 8k resolution.
```

---

### 15. `ad-story` — Stories / Reels (вертикальный 9:16)
**Суть:** Вертикальный формат для Instagram/TikTok.

**Промпт (ВЕРТИКАЛЬНЫЙ формат!):**
```
Create a vertical STORY/REELS advertising creative (9:16 format). Product: trendy sunglasses. Product centered in the middle third of the frame, slightly enlarged, with a soft glow behind it. Headline "SUMMER VIBES" bold condensed white sans-serif in the top quarter, drop shadow. Background: vibrant vertical gradient (sky blue to coral). Subtle bokeh effects. Strong rim light creating a glowing silhouette. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution. Optimized for mobile viewing.
```

## 🃏 КАРТОЧКА (вкладка "Карточка")

### 16. `card` — Инфографика для маркетплейса
**Суть:** Карточка товара с выносками, иконками, текстом — как на WB/Ozon.

**Промпт (классический стиль):**
```
Create a professional product card for marketplace. Product: robot vacuum cleaner. Classic style: light white background, clean typography, bright accents. Product centered or left (40% width), cleanly cut out with shadow. Around it: 3-5 infographic callouts with icons and short text (e.g. "Smart mapping", "3h battery", "Ultra quiet"). Title: large bold font at top. Bottom: pill badges with specs. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution.
```

**Промпт (премиум стиль):**
```
Create a professional product card for marketplace. Product: premium coffee machine. Premium style: dark background, gold accents, elegant typography. Product centered, cleanly cut out with golden shadow. 3-5 infographic callouts with metallic icons and refined text. Title in gold gradient. Photorealistic, ultra-sharp focus, 8k resolution.
```
